Welcome!

Welcome to the book site of Analyzing Financial and Economic Data with R, second edition. Here you will find the full content of the book for the first seven chapters, as well as all end-of-chapter exercises.

The book is available for purchase in Amazon as an ebook, paperback or hardcover. You can find more free content, including data files and exercise solutions in this link. The online version is also available for download as a pdf or ebook1 file.

If you liked the material and are using the book, please consider purchasing it and leaving your feedback at Amazon. Your opinion is very important for promoting the book and help others learn more about R and RStudio. As an author, I certainly appreciate the gesture and will take it as a motivating factor for future editions.

About the Author

I’m an associate professor at the post-graduate program of Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), south of Brazil. I’m also the developer of several popular CRAN packages such as BatchGetSymbols. Further details about my academic work can be found in my personal site.

Every end of year I write about what happened during the yearly cycle and what’s to come.

This year was very special and impactful for me. I learned a lot about myself, about what I do and why. Here are the highlights.

Highlights of 2022 and 2021

Academic Papers

I published and co-authored three academic papers during this period:

  • What drives the release of material facts for Brazilian stocks?
  • The Academic Inbreeding Controversy: Analysis and Evidence from Brazil
  • Board gender diversity: performance and risk of Brazilian firms

Books

  • The first edition of book Visualização de Dados com o R was published in October 2022. This was a great project and I had a great time and learned a lot doing it.
  • The third edition of Análise de Dados Financeiros e Econômicos com o R was published.

R Packages

Package yfR is the new iteration of BatchGetSymbols, with several new features and improvements. I had a very good experience submitting it for publication in ropensci. Both reviewers had great ideas that were implemented.
